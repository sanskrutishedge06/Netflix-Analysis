# 🎬 Netflix Content Strategy Analysis

Welcome to my Netflix Data Analysis project! In this project, I explored a dataset containing over 8,800 movies and TV shows available on Netflix to uncover how the streaming giant has changed its content strategy over the years.

📌 Project Overview
The goal of this project was to move beyond just looking at a list of movies and actually visualize the "big picture." I focused on:
* Content Distribution:** Understanding the balance between Movies and TV Shows.
* Growth Trends:** Seeing how much content Netflix adds each year.
* Global Hubs:** Identifying which countries are the biggest producers of Netflix content.

📊 Key Insights
* Movies vs. TV Shows:** Despite the popularity of series, Movies still make up the majority of the Netflix library.
* Content Explosion:** There was a massive spike in content added between 2016 and 2021.
* Top Producers:** The United States and India lead the world in content production for the platform.
